About Us

Get to knows us


Donate to Cause a Smile

    Legacy Support Uganda is a non-profit organization committed to making a positive impact on the lives of vulnerable children, the elderly, and those in need in Uganda. Our mission is to provide essential services, resources, and advocacy to these communities, fostering a culture of care, kindness, and opportunity.

Our Vision

    We strive to create a compassionate and inclusive society where everyone, regardless of their circumstances, can thrive. Our vision is to bridge gaps in support, empower individuals, and build a stronger, more equitable community.

Advocacy: We advocate for policies and programs that benefit vulnerable populations and promote social justice.

Dedicated


Our Mission

    • Provide essential services: We offer a range of services, including education, healthcare, nutrition, and shelter, to meet the immediate needs of those we serve.
    • Empower individuals: We empower individuals through education, skills training, and mentorship, enabling them to reach their full potential.
    • Advocate for change: We advocate for policies and practices that promote social justice, equality, and the well-being of vulnerable populations.
    • Foster community: We build strong communities by fostering relationships, promoting volunteerism, and encouraging participation in local initiatives.

Our Programs

    • Child Sponsorship: Our child sponsorship program provides essential support to children in need, including education, healthcare, and nutrition.
    • Community Development: We implement projects that address long-term challenges, such as improving sanitation, providing clean water, and promoting sustainable agriculture.
    • Elderly Care: We offer support services to the elderly, including healthcare, meals, and companionship.

Perfect People


Our Impact

    Through our programs and initiatives, we have made a significant impact on the lives of countless individuals in Uganda. Our work has helped to:
    • Improve education: We provide educational opportunities for children, ensuring they have access to quality education and a brighter future.
    • Enhance healthcare: We support healthcare initiatives, ensuring that individuals have access to necessary medical care.
    • Address poverty: We address poverty by providing essential resources and support to those in need.
    • Strengthen communities: We foster strong communities by promoting social cohesion, volunteerism, and community development.

Why Support Legacy Support Uganda?

    By supporting Legacy Support Uganda, you are making a direct impact on the lives of vulnerable individuals in Uganda. Your generosity can help break the cycle of poverty, empower children to become leaders, and create a more equitable and just world.

Join Us

    We invite you to become a part of our mission. Whether you choose to volunteer your time, donate to our cause, or advocate for our work, your support will make a difference in the lives of those we serve. Together, we can build a more compassionate and inclusive society for all.
